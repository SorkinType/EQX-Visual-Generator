Cucumis melo var. conomon Mak.

WEIGHT: 14 ounces
SUGAR CONTENT: 6° Brix
MATURITY: Early
SEED SOURCE: Horus

Thought to be the Oh-gon 9 gou melon bred in 1931 at the Chiba Agricultural Experiment Station in Japan. Similar to other makuwa uri. This type of melon is a gold mine for plant breeders searching for resistance to pests and diseases such as Cucumber Mosaic Virus and Melon Necrotic Spot Virus.
